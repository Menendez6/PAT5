About the job

Are you a career Project Manager with a real passion for driving change? Do you have a flair for energising and focusing delivery teams to meet and exceed customer expectations, and have the confidence to engage with technical experts to deliver great outcomes? Do you believe in leading from the front and leading by example? If so we’d like to hear from you.

We have a number of opportunities for ambitious Project Managers to join our teams that deliver transformational change, and systems implementations - across our customer base. Playing a key role on our mission to bring connectivity to every part of the UK, you’ll be joining Cellnex at a time of digital innovation in support of areas including Smart Cities, IoT, digital broadcasting and the UK’s emergency services. If you’re a Project Management professional who’s excited by the proposition of taking a lead role in enabling communications infrastructure for the 21st century we want to hear from you.


MAIN FUNCTIONS:
  • Identify the project scope, goals and deliverables to support the project delivery.
  • Work with the customer, delivery teams, internal stakeholders and suppliers to ensure we’re meeting and exceeding customer expectations.
  • Identify the task and resources required to deliver the project and produce a project plan to outline timelines, dependencies and critical paths.
